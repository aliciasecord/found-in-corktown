Point system

5 points for a current photo submission +1 for every year that has passed

10 points for a current video submission +3 for every year that has passed

5 points for stories from the past 5 year, 10 points for stories from 6-10 years ago, 20 points for story from 11-30 years ago, 30 points for story from more than 30 years ago

Photo/video must be taken by the person who is submitting it. Submitter must have direct knowledge of stories being shared.

Points are tallied daily and the person with the most points on March 31 will win.

about the adventure

This is a digital scavenger hunt to engage the residents, businesses, and visitors of Corktown in uncovering the stories and histories of Detroit's oldest neighborhood. Submitted entries will be used to inform an ongoing series of sponsored courses at the College for Creative Studies.

Enter the contest by using #foundincorktown for photos and videos on Twitter or Instagram or by uploading photos or video to this site with your name and email address. Click here to sign up for updates. By submitting your photos, videos, and stories, you agree to their use by the students of the College for Creative Studies in Detroit.

For questions, email foundincorktown@gmail.com.

Ford + College for Creative Studies + Corktown

This project is part of an ongoing series of sponsored studio courses which aim to:

  • Visualize the impact of the Ford's presence on the city and the future of mobility
  • Research Corktown’s history, residents, and ethnography
  • Explore geographic and architectural landmarks
  • Collaborate with Ford to create futuristic, visual experiences to document Ford’s Corktown Campus

To contact the class about other projects or concerns, drop us a line at FCT@collegeforcreativestudies.edu.
